# IKA Car Design Context

## Users

**Target audience:** Private car sellers, car buyers, and car dealers in Denmark.

**Context:** Users come to Autoværdi when they're curious about their car's market value or preparing to negotiate a sale/purchase. They may be at home researching, at a dealership comparing prices, or on-the-go using their phone.

**Job to be done:** Get an accurate, trustworthy car valuation instantly without hassle. The tool should remove uncertainty and empower confident decision-making.

## Brand Personality

**Voice & tone:** Precise, confident, understated luxury. The interface speaks with authority but doesn't shout. Every interaction feels deliberate and refined.

**3-word personality:** Premium, Trustworthy, Intelligent

**Emotional goals:** After using Autoværdi, users should feel:

- **Confident** — they have reliable data to back their decisions
- **Relieved** — the process was effortless and transparent
- **Empowered** — they understand their position in the market
- **Surprised** — by the quality and speed of the experience

## Aesthetic Direction

**Visual tone:** Premium minimalism with intentional sophistication. Clean lines, generous whitespace, refined typography. Think Linear.com — interfaces that feel expensive without being flashy.

**References:**

- **Linear.app** — masterclass in premium minimalism; subtle depth, purposeful motion, sophisticated color usage

**Anti-references:**

- Playful/toy-like interfaces
- Clinical/medical coldness
- Boring/corporate sameness
- Neon/oversaturated colors
- Generic dashboard aesthetics

**Theme:** Both light and dark modes. Default to user's system preference. Light mode for daytime trustworthiness, dark mode for evening sophistication.

## Design Principles

1. **Precision over decoration** — Every element earns its place. No decorative flourishes that don't serve function.

2. **Confidence through restraint** — Bold choices in spacing, typography, and color create an impression of expertise. Understated beats loud.

3. **Intelligence in the details** — Micro-interactions, transitions, and states should feel considered and smooth. The interface should feel alive but never busy.

4. **Trust through transparency** — Information hierarchy should make complex data feel approachable. No hidden complexity.

5. **Accessible by default** — Respect user preferences for motion. Design works beautifully with or without animations.

6. **Avoid the AI palette** — No cyan-on-dark, no purple gradients, no neon accents. Sophisticated, muted tones with purposeful contrast.
